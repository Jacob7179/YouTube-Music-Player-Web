About YouTube Music Player
YouTube Music Player

A feature-rich web-based music player that uses YouTube as its music source. Play, manage, and organize your favorite music in a clean, intuitive interface.

Features
  • YouTube music playback
  • Playlist management with drag & drop
  • Lyrics display with auto-sync
  • Lyrics translation
  • Dark/Light mode
  • Export/Import playlists
  • Volume control & progress bar
  • Multi-language support (English/中文)
  • Auto-play & repeat modes
